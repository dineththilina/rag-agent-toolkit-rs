# Helios Robotics — Company Overview

Helios Robotics is a fictional company used to demonstrate retrieval-augmented
generation in the agent-toolkit project. All facts below are invented so the
agent has a self-contained, verifiable knowledge base to answer from.

## Founding

Helios Robotics was founded in 2019 in Lisbon, Portugal by Mara Quintero and
Daniel Osei. The company started as a three-person team building warehouse
navigation software and grew to 220 employees by 2025.

## Mission

The mission of Helios Robotics is to make autonomous indoor logistics affordable
for mid-sized warehouses that cannot justify the cost of traditional industrial
automation. The company sells a combination of hardware (the Helios H1 robot)
and a subscription software platform (Helios Fleet).

## Headquarters

The headquarters is located in Lisbon, with a secondary engineering office in
Krakow, Poland and a sales office in Austin, Texas. Manufacturing is outsourced
to a contract partner in Porto.

## Funding

Helios Robotics raised a 4 million euro seed round in 2020 and an 18 million
euro Series A in 2023, led by the fictional fund Northwind Ventures. The company
has not raised a Series B as of 2025.
