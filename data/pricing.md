# Helios Fleet — Pricing and Plans

Helios Fleet is the subscription software platform that manages a fleet of
Helios H1 robots. The pricing below is fictional and used only for demos.

## Plans

There are three subscription tiers, all billed per robot per month.

### Starter
The Starter plan costs 90 euros per robot per month. It includes fleet
monitoring, basic route planning, and email support. It supports up to 5 robots
per warehouse.

### Growth
The Growth plan costs 160 euros per robot per month. It adds multi-floor
coordination, traffic optimization, an analytics dashboard, and chat support.
It supports up to 30 robots per warehouse.

### Premium
The Premium plan costs 240 euros per robot per month. It adds next-business-day
replacement parts, a dedicated account manager, custom integrations, and a
99.9 percent uptime guarantee. There is no robot limit.

## Discounts

Annual prepayment receives a 15 percent discount on any plan. Customers
deploying more than 50 robots qualify for custom enterprise pricing negotiated
directly with the Helios sales office in Austin.

## Trial

New customers get a 30-day free trial of the Growth plan, limited to 3 robots.
No payment details are required to start the trial.
