Helios H1 Robot — Product Specifications

The Helios H1 is the flagship autonomous mobile robot from Helios Robotics.
The specifications below are fictional and exist to support retrieval demos.

Dimensions and weight
The H1 measures 80 cm long, 55 cm wide, and 40 cm tall. It weighs 48 kilograms
without a payload.

Payload
The maximum payload capacity is 120 kilograms. The recommended continuous
operating payload is 90 kilograms for best battery life.

Battery and runtime
The H1 uses a 1.2 kWh lithium iron phosphate battery. On a full charge it runs
for approximately 9 hours under typical warehouse load. A full recharge takes
90 minutes using the Helios Dock charging station.

Speed
The maximum travel speed is 2.0 meters per second. In zones marked as
human-shared, firmware limits the speed to 1.2 meters per second.

Sensors
The H1 has a 2D LiDAR unit for navigation, four depth cameras for obstacle
detection, and an inertial measurement unit. It does not have a robotic arm;
loading and unloading is handled by conveyor integrations or human operators.

Connectivity
The robot connects over Wi-Fi 6 to the Helios Fleet platform. If the network
drops, the robot completes its current task and then waits at the nearest safe
parking zone until connectivity returns.

Warranty
Every H1 ships with a 24-month hardware warranty. Helios Fleet subscribers on
the Premium tier receive next-business-day replacement parts.
