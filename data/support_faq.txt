Helios Fleet — Support FAQ

These fictional support answers give the retrieval agent factual content to
ground its responses.

Q: How do I add a new robot to my fleet?
A: Open the Helios Fleet dashboard, go to Fleet > Devices, and click "Pair
Device". Scan the QR code on the back of the H1 robot. The robot appears in your
fleet within about 30 seconds once it connects to Wi-Fi.

Q: What happens if a robot loses Wi-Fi during a task?
A: The robot finishes its current task, then drives to the nearest safe parking
zone and waits. It resumes automatically when the network returns. No data is
lost because tasks are cached locally on the robot.

Q: Can I run robots on more than one floor?
A: Multi-floor coordination is available on the Growth and Premium plans. You
must map each floor separately and configure the elevator integration. The
Starter plan is single-floor only.

Q: How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
A: Plan changes are made under Billing > Subscription in the dashboard. Upgrades
take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing
cycle so you are not charged twice.

Q: What is the uptime guarantee?
A: Only the Premium plan includes a contractual uptime guarantee, set at 99.9
percent measured monthly. If Helios fails to meet it, affected customers receive
service credits.

Q: Who do I contact for hardware failures?
A: Premium subscribers get next-business-day replacement parts and should
contact their dedicated account manager. Starter and Growth customers open a
ticket through the dashboard support panel.
